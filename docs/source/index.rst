Welcome to RUC-MESSAGEix-China (RMC) documentation!
====================================================

The RUC-MESSAGEix-China (RMC) model is a provincial-scale energy-economy-environment (E3) integrated assessment model for China based on the open-source modelling framework MESSAGEix `IIASA ECE Programme, 2020 <references.html>`_. It was co-developed by Prof. Zhou Wenji’s group at the School of Applied Economics, Renmin University of China and Prof. Ren Hongtao’s group at the School of Business, East China University of Science and Technology.
